Ragtags

Tabletop Role-Playing Game

What is Ragtags?
Ragtags is a game about light-hearted, low-stakes adventures, where a group of intrepid heroes take on foes of all shapes and sizes to carry on their quest. These quests range from silly little adventures, like capturing an elusive goose, to defeating a fearsome monster that has been terrorizing the local village, which is probably also a goose.

What makes Ragtags different?

Low stakes

While the adventures you'll embark on with this system can be serious, it's a far cry from the more world-endy types of narratives that permeate the rest of the fantasy genre.

Simple, but deep

Ragtags is both simple to get into and run while remaining deep and engaging. You can start playing minutes after picking up the rulebook.

It's free!

While you can pick up the illustrated rulebook for $4.95, the bare bones edition is free to download and contains all the rules!

What do I need to play?

Subtitle

You'll need the rules. Can't play the game without them, now, can we?

You'll need some friends. We recommend up to four.

You'll need two 12-sided polyhedral dice (2d12)

You'll need printed copies of the character sheet. One for each player.
